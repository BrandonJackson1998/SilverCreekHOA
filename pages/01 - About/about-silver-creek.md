# Silver Creek Townhomes — St. George, Utah

1732 West 540 North, St. George, UT 84770

37.119232, -113.620482  
Google Maps PlusCode: 499H+5Q, St. George, UT  

Between Westridge and Dixie Drive in the City of St. George, Utah.

There are no through streets in Silver Creek, so the traffic flow is light and quiet.

Silver Creek is two miles or less from 3 large grocery stores, 40 restaurants, 8 banks, medical/dental/ophthalmic/veterinary offices, 4 pharmacies, a multiplex theater, Home Depot, 8 parks, 2 golf courses, a large public Aquatic Center, all 4 Public Schools (Pre-K through High), and many churches.
